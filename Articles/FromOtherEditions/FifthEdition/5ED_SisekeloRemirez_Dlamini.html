Young and Called: Inside the Spiritual Tabernacle of Ramires!

By Dallas Day

Last edited: December 19, 2023

Ever smiling, loving, born leader, visionary, young and commissioned. These are some of the words that can be instrumentalized to describe the Student Christian Movement chairperson Sisekelo ‘Ramires’ Dlamini. The nickname comes after former Chelsea midfield maestro Ramires Do Santos Nascimento who became a European champion in 2012. Like Ramires, Sisekelo is fierce in his championship of spreading the good news of the gospel to the world arena.

Background

Sisekelo was raised at Mpolonjeni in Siteki in the Lubombo region, he did his primary and High School at ST Philips. He the completed his Matric in the republic of South Africa at Bambanani interestingly that is where he turned a new leaf and unapologetically received Jesus Christ as his saviour. Since then, he has never looked back. In fact, he is a living testimony of the scripture which says “remember your creator in the days of your youth, before the days of trouble come and the years approach when you will say, I find no pleasure in them’’

SCM Presidency

Literature stalwart; William Shakespeare once casted a powerful statement positing that “some are born great, some achieve greatness, some have greatness thrust upon them’’. Shakespeare hands down could have been referring to Ramires in all the essences of the statement. His spiritual grand enlightenment dawned while he was in his first year at University. Through numerous visions, he was shown that one fateful day he will become the SCM president. This vision became a reality on his third year which shows that God still does communicate with his own. During the long- awaited day his predecessor Nkululeko Shongwe when doing the much- anticipated handover, he proudly named him ‘JOSHUA’ as the chosen one to lead the mighty movement with dignity to the land of milk and honey!! what a memorable moment it was. That seemed to be prophetic as he was elected the coordinator of UNESWA SCM meaning he is the president not just in Kwaluseni but in all three campuses some indeed he is the pillar of SCM. Since his takeover the movement has been the centre of vibrancy, spiritually reviving concerts like the consuming fire musical concert which was a long forgotten SCM concert last hosted six years ago but through God and his dedicated committee they brought it back to life. What a journey from being Scripture Union’s additional member in Grade 12 to become SCM coordinator in Varsity!!

LOVED BY GOD

Paul spread the gospel through missionary journeys, Jesus Christ through miracles and parables while Remires and his friends spreads the message of God’s love through clothing. It goes without any iota of doubt that when many people think about the clothing brand ‘LOVED BY GOD’ they associate it with Sisekelo , more like those who firmly holds the view that Desmond Dube owns Clientele. The similarity of the two is that they are both excellent brand ambassadors who put their bodies on the line to ensure the success and popularity of the brands that they represent. Loved By God is actually owned by his friend Mzwakhe, launched in 2020 but he has since made it a household brand in the University structures and Christian family. I am definitely sure that if a census was to be run in UNESWA over 30 % of students dons this brand with outmost love. The brand has been selling like hotcakes hence it is now more accurate to call it an international brand as it is found in South Africa and Mozambique. The aspiration is for the brand to have it’s own stalls and not only specialize in spreading awareness of God’s love through clothing but through other wings which are going to be revealed soon.

WORDS OF WISDOM

He may not be Steve Harvey but his top-notch advice to the youth is that “young people, you are not going to be young forever, you maybe young today but you will be old tomorrow, be the next leader of your generation, so every decision you make today has a direct implication or result of our future. Be careful of the decision you make today as it has direct impact on your future. It is wise to make sound decisions while still young, youthful and energetic because when you mess up with your life right now those scars will remain with you forever. The beginning of wisdom is to fear God, if you want to strive in life seek wisdom” That is the presidential advice to the youth inspired by God’s Love. Ramires further urged those who stay around campus to attend SCM services as they protect one from temptations, peer pressure and keep your spiritual life in tact.
