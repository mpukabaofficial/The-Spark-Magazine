Urcousinphayo: Balancing Success in the Music Industry

By Lusanda King

Last edited: September 16, 2023

From the moment we are born, we begin to dream. Some dreams change with time, while others remain a constant. But for all of us, there are those dreams that speak to our very soul - the ones we know we must pursue, no matter what obstacles may stand in our way. While not all of us are given the chance to see our dreams realized, those who do experience a sense of fulfillment unlike any other.

Nkosephayo Ntokozo Mndzebele, born and raised in Swaziland, is making waves in multiple industries. From his early years in Manzini, Swaziland, to pursuing his education in Schoemansdal, Mpumalanga, Nkosephayo's journey has been one of determination and passion. After completing his high school education, he went on to obtain a Law degree from Nelson Mandela University in Gqeberha (formerly known as Port Elizabeth).Throughout his journey, Nkosephayo's biggest supporter has been his mother. With unwavering love and belief in her son, she has carried him through challenging situations. Initially, she struggled to understand his passion for deejaying, but she eventually warmed up to it, asking about his gigs and expressing immense excitement when he appeared in newspapers.

As a DJ, Nkosephayo faced the challenge of building his name and gaining recognition in the competitive entertainment industry. Understanding the importance of relevance in securing bookings, he tirelessly worked towards establishing himself. To balance his professional life as a DJ and entrepreneur, he carefully allocates time between his DJing gigs, which predominantly happen during weekends and nights, and his business ventures, which operate during regular working hours. Nkosephayo's stage name, URCOUSINPHAYO, symbolizes the close connection he feels with his listeners and supporters. He considers them as his extended family and proudly embraces the nickname "Mzala we Khantri." Rather than viewing them as fans, Nkosephayo values the sense of community and belonging that comes with a shared love for music.

His love for music developed from a young age, as he fondly recalls his first experience with music through a mini radio that played cassettes. He and his family would charge the batteries with solar power so they could enjoy listening to shows like "Talabasha netabo" on EBIS 1 radio. This early exposure to the enchanting sounds of music ignited Nkosephayo's passion for becoming a DJ.While talent and skills are crucial for success as a DJ, Nkosephayo emphasizes the significance of positioning oneself for opportunities through effective marketing. Utilizing social media and the internet, he has harnessed the power of self-promotion, gaining bookings even in locations where he hasn't physically performed. The accessibility provided by social media has proven to be a game-changer for his career.

As an afro house DJ, Nkosephayo actively follows various afro house producers and pages to stay updated on exclusive content. Moreover, his friendships with these producers enable him to receive new songs for testing purposes. This collaboration benefits both parties and contributes to the diversification of his sound. Nkosephayo rarely experiences performance anxiety, but when he does, he relies on his support structure to remind him of his talent and abilities. To overcome creative blocks, he explores different genres, occasionally finding inspiration from amapiano or vocal elements to infuse into his preferred genres of afro house and afro tech.

Though mindful of industry politics, Nkosephayo chooses to ignore them, focusing instead on his work and avoiding unnecessary stress. He acknowledges that the industry's dynamics can indirectly affect him as an artist, but he remains committed to his craft and keeps his focus on making great music. The most rewarding aspect of being a DJ for Nkosephayo is witnessing the joy on people's faces when his song selection resonates with them. He believes that experiencing someone moved to tears by a song signifies the true impact of being a DJ. Additionally, traveling to various venues nationwide has exposed him to places he never knew existed, enriching his cultural experiences.

Nkosephayo's talent and hard work have not gone unnoticed. In 2022, he was a top 5 finalist in the popular DJ category at the Social Media Awards hosted by Swazi Boy TV. With confidence and determination, he aims to surpass his achievements this year. Recent performances alongside renowned artists such as Kwesta, Maglera doe boy, Blxckie, Da Capo, and Sun El Musician have further solidified Nkosephayo's presence in the industry. Such opportunities have allowed him to showcase his skills while collaborating with established names.Looking ahead, Nkosephayo aspires to share the stage with Morda, admiring his unique playing style and his new sound since his departure from Black Motion. He is also eager to perform in the Netherlands and Spain, where the love for house music is vibrant.

Nkosephayo Ntokozo Mndzebele continues to inspire with his multifaceted talents as a DJ, lawyer, and entrepreneur. His journey exemplifies the possibilities that arise when passion and dedication converge, serving as an inspiration to aspiring artists in the entertainment industry. With his distinctive sound and unwavering determination, Nkosephayo is destined for even greater success in the future.

Born from his passion for fashion and the desire to create garments that stood out from the rest, SKOON DRIP is making waves in the industry, slowly but steadily. Nkosephayo's journey began with his previous clothing brand, Since Van Tuka (SVT). However, he soon discovered that the name was widely used by other clothing brands, leading to a lack of exclusivity. That's when he made the decision to close SVT and give birth to SKOON DRIP, striving to create a clean and minimalistic brand.

The name SKOON DRIP reflects the brand's vision and aesthetic. "SKOON" is an Afrikaans term that epitomizes cleanliness, purity, and undiluted style, while "DRIP" is a slang word synonymous with swag and fashion. This combination perfectly encapsulates the brand's intention to create sleek and minimalistic designs, evident in their new logo. SKOON DRIP products often feature small print logos, maintaining the brand's clean and sophisticated look. Unlike some competitors who rely on buying pre-made garments and branding them, SKOON DRIP stands out by creating its own garments from carefully selected high-quality materials. The brand's emphasis on detail ensures a perfect finish, resonating strongly with their clients' taste and preferences.

Among their range, the RARE line has become a customer favorite, especially their sweaters. These exclusive designs caught people's attention with their uniquely placed prints and top-notch material quality. The SKOON DRIP OG tee is also highly popular, offering simplicity with a strong fashion statement. Staying true to their ethos, SKOON DRIP paves its own path and doesn't try to follow trends or compete with other brands. By moving at their own pace and maintaining their unique style, they have established themselves as one of the top brands in Eswatini, even two years after their inception.

The experience of running SKOON DRIP has been nothing short of amazing for Nkosephayo. Personally delivering the products allows him to see the joy on customers' faces in real-time and hear their valuable feedback. This feedback helps the brand grow and ensures they consistently offer the best products. Looking ahead, Nkosephayo envisions SKOON DRIP becoming the number one brand in Swaziland within the next two years. The team is currently working behind the scenes to launch a commercial website, providing customers with the convenience of ordering their favorite SKOON DRIP products directly to their doorstep or a nearby town. In the next five years, or even sooner, SKOON DRIP aims to open its own flagship store, offering customers a memorable shopping experience with unparalleled quality and exceptional customer service.

When it comes to inspiration, Nkosephayo draws from his own life experiences and his unique perspective of the world. The RARE edition, for example, serves as a reminder to individuals that they are rare gems who should embrace their true selves. Nkosephayo wants to encourage his clients to be authentic and unique, because there is only one of them.In terms of other clothing brands that he admires, GALXBOY and FEAR OF GOD top Nkosephayo's list. However, it is football legend Teko Modise who inspires him most in the realm of fashion.

SKOON DRIP's innovative approach to fashion presents a refreshing take on style and individuality. With Nkosephayo's determination and creativity at the helm, SKOON DRIP is poised for continued growth and success, captivating fashion lovers with their one-of-a-kind designs.

Nkosephayo's story is an inspiration to us all. He proves that with hard work, dedication, and the support of loved ones, we can achieve our goals, no matter what obstacles we face. So don't let your fears or doubts hold you back - take a page from Nkosephayo's book and go after your dreams with everything you've got. The sky's the limit, and your possibilities are endless. Remember, your journey is your own, but you're never alone.
