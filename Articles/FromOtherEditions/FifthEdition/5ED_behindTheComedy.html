BEHIND THE COMEDY: Exploring the Creative Royalty of SLWANE!

From the Spark Editorial Desk: Mlandvo- Sikhumbuzo Mavimbela)

Last edited: September 26, 2023

First word I think of whenever the name of this formidable man comes up is “pioneer”. In every sense this man is a creative trendsetter whose impeccable contribution in the arts industry of the country has revolutionised the game in its own distinct right. A peerless custodian of astounding entertainment who has kept audiences on the edges of their seats with their jaws on the floor with laughter over the years. Known for his matchless “Gogo and Slwane” skits, Ncamiso Ntshingila with an impressive creative arts catalogue has made a name for himself as a sought-after comedian, writer, producer, director, actor and dancer. This article pursues to unveil the mask off the face of this comedy powerhouse and voyages around the experiences and highlights of his laudable career with marginal insights on his personal life.

From setting global stages ablaze with his nonpareil theatrical performances to sharing comedic stages with the likes of Celeste Ntuli, to starring in the most successful film the country has ever produced to date, Slwane has always been an entertainer from a very young age. “My former schoolmates always remind me of how wayward I was in school”, he reminisces of how he has always been “the funny guy” for as long as he can remember. Growing up in the small community of Matara in Ngwenya, Ntshingila was raised by a single mother and he attributes his upbringing to the iconic man he has turned out to be today. “Growing up without my father made me decide that I wanted to be a present father to my kids”, the father of one beautiful baby girl enunciates on his core principles as a present father in his daughter’s life. You cannot begin to even ignore the warmth on his voice the very moment he speaks of his daughter. “She changed my life”, the comedian beaming with pride says of his daughter, crediting her for ameliorating his life and making him see life from different lenses. With family being his arch support system, Ncamiso acknowledges his mother for supporting his dream of pursuing a career in a rather winding creative arts landscape in the country.

The beginning of the voyage: PELEPELE CULTURAL GROUP

The atomic genesis of Ncamiso’s story begins at Ngwenya Village, where his family moved to in his teenage years. The inception seed of his career was sworn when he joined a peer educator programme at Ngwenya Town. Being a part of the programme as a peer educator supervisor exposed Ncamiso to an entirely different world that changed his life forever. The doors of the alluring tapestry of drama were opened to him through a series of drama workshops, the programme offered. That is when Ncamiso first tasted the euphoric illusion of performance and since then he never again wanted to ever sober up from the ecstasy the art of drama and performance gave him. It was during this creative trance that he met with industry giants including entertainment entrepreneur and artist manager Larry Mhlanga who was a key catalyst in opening international doors for Ncamiso through the globally renowned; Pelepele dance group. What Larry Mhlanga conceptualized initially as a Marimba band grew to become unarguably the best dance and cultural arts group the country has every seen. Promoted to being Larry’s second-in-command as an assistant director of the group, Ncamiso alongside his Pelepele crew members had a medley of successful International sold-out tours in Europe, Dubai and USA between 2013-2014. Pelepele was also selected to participate in an international dance competition in South Korea with an over E5 Million prize at stake. The group enjoyed a successful international footprint and revolutionized the entertainment scene in Eswatini. The two-time National Arts and Culture Awars winning group was in every sense an academy and an incubation ground for many young creatives in the country whom have gone on to have illustrious individual careers. One of these creatives is famed comedian: SMALLZ who has gone on to enjoy cross-border success too.

UMJINGI UDLIWA YINHLITIYO

A principled theatre practitioner with years of intense stage acting training, Ncamiso’s next move was the big screen. The year 2015 saw him land a leading role in the acclaimed Mzansi-Magic produced movie: Umjingi Udliwa Inhlitiyo, a tale of forbidden love based on the novel by seasoned writer: Sijabulile Nsibandze. A part he was reluctant in auditioning for, he never could have predetermined how successful the film would turn out to be. Starring as the funny hopeless romantic: “Lusekwane”, he had instant chemistry with onscreen romance flame, Hloniphile Ndzimande. The connection and chemistry the had in the movie could remind one of the chemistry Leonardo DiCaprio and Kate Winslet had in the movie: Titanic. With acting being somewhat his nature, Ncamiso had zero struggles from adapting from his usual niche: theatre performance to performing in an entirely different acting sphere, film. Infact in his true “Jack of All Trades and Master of them all” nature, Ncamiso recalls how he was a one-take wonder throughout the shooting of the movie much to director: Modison Magagula’s amazement. “The director was blown away with how I knew the whole script and how I never struggled with my lines”, Ncamiso reminiscences of his time in the what is undoubtably the first Swati film to reach BoxOffice success of matchless magnitude . He stresses the importance of telling our own stories to authentic accord,unapologetically so! With a slowly developing arts landscape, Ncamiso implores the government to put its money where its mouth is in fast tracking the progression of the struggling industry. The film remains one of Slwane’s highlights in the creative fraternity.

A MAN BEHIND THE SCENES: The birth of GOGO and SLWANE

Ncamiso served as the creative engine behind comedy giant: SMALLZ THE COMEDIAN designing his skits and writing his comedic monologues. Ncamiso’s contribution to SMALLZ’s career availed him to participate and win the coveted LOL comedy search competition which quickly asserted him as a comedy household name. What is incontestably the most monumental career move the comedy eccentric has taken to date is being a part of the peerless comedy trio: “The Merchants of Bottomless Laughter” consisting of SMALLZ, himself and Mduduzi Nkhambule ( “GOGO”). The trio were not about to fathom the leaps their comedy skits would take them to. The ensemble quickly rose to fame making it the most successful comedy group in the country to date. Ncamiso continued to utilize the scriptwriting experience he has in formulating skits for the ensemble. With Facebook video uploads garnering hundreds of thousands of views, the Bottomless Laughter trio begun to enjoy prominence in neighbouring countries like South Africa, setting South Africa comedy stages alight!

The departure of SMALZZ from the ensemble in 2021 did not deter GOGO and SLWANE from constantly pushing the envelope in the comedy sphere. The GOGO and SLWANE brand is the leading marque in as far as comedy is concerted in the country. Known for being trendsetters of note and a marketer’s biggest dream, the “GOGO&SLWANE” brand is easily the ultimate apple of the eye of many corporate companies at the moment right now. This is evident through the duo’s partnership and brand-ambassadorship with big players in corporate including United Holdings and Nedbank to name a few. It is safe to say that Ncamiso has learnt something that most performers in the country fail to comprehend: the art of understanding the business side of the game! The management of the group by Nzimeni Mpanza has also been one of the chief ingredients in driving the brand to success. Ncamiso acknowledges the fact that the brand has grown in leaps and bounds to become a beast bigger than them and their minimal fathoming.

The level of incomprehensible love Ncamiso has for his duo partner is insanely heartening. He cannot stop raving about the unrivalled talent Mduduzi has. He without any doubt aprimmes Gogo as the most talented creative he has ever worked with his entire career. The relationship between the two comedy heavyweights transcends the walls of a comedy arena, according to Ncamiso, they have become more than just colleagues and business partners. “I’d like to say keep pushing man, the sky is the limit even if my presence is not with you one day, YOU ARE WHAT THE WORLD NEEDS”, said Ncamiso in a big brotherly tone when I asked him to share any message, he has always wanted to convey to Mduduzi.

The Future

As an individual arts practitioner, Ncamiso wants to continue stretching the fringe. He is currently working on a movie titled: “Nguye Longuye”, a movie he wrote and directed himself, starring Mduduzi “GOGO” Nkhambule as part of the cast. This amplifies the volume on the level of artistic maturity this seasoned thespian and comedian is on and his future seems to be dominated by a lot of creative growth.

Known to be one not selfish on giving his nuggets of experienced wisdom to aspiring art creatives, Ncamiso is passionate about contributing to the growing of the arts sector in grassroots level. He is constantly invited to art development workshops around the country where he curates and lends his artistic advice budding performers. To the aspiring Ncamiiso somewhere in Nkiliji or Lavumisa with dreams of achieving renowned successes in the arts medium as Ncamiso has, the seasoned artist warns the young and upcoming artists against the dreaded demon called “fame”. According to Ncamiso one has to obsess with perfecting the craft to the pinnacle of their abilities before chasing fame. Despite all the fame Ncamiso in still in touch with his spiritual clinch, serving as a conductor and chorister amongst other duties in his spiritual home: The Old Apostolic Church. A fun fact that most people apart from his fellow church members are clueless in knowing is his love for singing and serving in the OPC Ministry.

A story of ambition and not letting your background define and hinder you from reaching your dreams, Ncamiso’s Spark is one that refuses to glow dimly but is in constant evolution. All eyes on him as he continues to be amongst the tier of individuals revolutionizing the creative arts terrain of the country. A pioneer of note who will go down as one of the people that laid the foundation work for the existence of performance arts in the country and broke ground for the pontification of comedy in the mainstream scene. A trailblazing entertainment legend of our time!
