Nhlonipho Dlamini: A Musical Journey from Swaziland to South Africa

Article by Lusanda King

Last edited: October 23, 2023

In the captivating world of entertainment, the path to success is seldom easy. Nhlonipho Dlamini, a proud Swazi-born artist, has embraced this challenge wholeheartedly. As a vocalist, pianist, and qualified civil engineer, Nhlonipho has dedicated his life to music, finding solace and fulfilment in his artistic pursuits. In this article, we delve into his experiences within the industry, examining the highs and lows, his support structure, and the remarkable journey he undertook to navigate through the unpredictable landscape of the entertainment industry.

Nhlonipho's love for music was not a sudden realization; instead, it was ingrained within him from an early age. Raised in a household where music permeated every corner, with his mother as a choral music conductor, Nhlonipho was surrounded by the enchantment of melodies and harmonies. His passion for music took hold during his primary school years, where he began his journey in choral music, embracing this art form and nurturing his unique talents.

Recognizing the challenges within the Eswatini art industry, Nhlonipho decided to spread his wings and seek new opportunities in South Africa. Despite holding a civil engineering qualification, his artistic calling resonated more strongly with him. With a burning desire to flourish as a musician, he took the courageous leap towards greener pastures, knowing that the path ahead would require perseverance and resilience. Despite facing challenges and having to convince his parents, he managed to gain their support and embark on a journey driven by passion and ambition. This marked the beginning of countless opportunities unfolding before him.

Every endeavour we undertake is fuelled by a motivating factor. While he was born into a family that valued music, what truly propelled him to pursue it as a career? During his final year of high school, he faced a suspension and had to stay home for a few weeks. Being a member of the choral music team, he utilized this time to study, learn, and train himself for an upcoming competition. Remarkably, he competed as a tenor soloist and emerged as the first-place winner. This event served as a turning point, making him realize the significant role music played in his life and how it fostered discipline.

Embarking on his journey in the highly competitive South African music scene, Nhlonipho faced his fair share of trials and tribulations. He encountered fierce competition, gruelling auditions, and numerous rejections. However, he persevered, drawing strength from his unwavering determination and a supportive network of friends, family, and mentors who believed in his talent and potential. It sounds like being in South Africa has had a significant impact on Nhlonipho's career as a musician. He recognized the importance of adjusting to the new environment and embracing the cultural differences. It seems that he approached the experience with a humble mindset, understanding that he needed to unlearn some things and start anew.

As Nhlonipho traversed the unpredictable landscape of the entertainment industry, he discovered the importance of adaptability and continuous growth. Cultivating his skills as a vocalist and pianist, he honed his craft, constantly evolving in his artistic expression. He embraced collaborations with fellow musicians and seized every opportunity to learn from industry professionals, refining his talent along the way. This willingness to learn and grow allowed him to develop as an artist. Additionally, being surrounded by other talented artists in South Africa seems to have motivated him to push himself further. Nhlonipho has had opportunities to collaborate with industry professionals like DJ Zinhle and share the stage with Aubrey Qwane, which has undoubtedly opened doors for his career.

It is commendable that Nhlonipho recognizes the importance of giving back to the society that nurtured his talent. He has expressed his desire to collaborate with Swazi artists in the future and actively engages with social media platforms to identify talented individuals such as Sandz, Mzwa, and Lyrical Busta. Despite the challenges posed by time management, particularly due to tight schedules, Nhlonipho is determined to find ways to fit in collaborative sessions and meetings. As a result, we can anticipate exciting collaborations to emerge in the near future.

Despite being located in South Africa, Nhlonipho has been able to showcase his incredible talent and contribute to his homeland. In 2022, he had the privilege of performing at the Standard Bank Luju festival in Swaziland. This experience on the Eswatini stage opened his eyes to the significant attention his work receives, which can sometimes be overlooked on social media platforms. Nhlonipho was grateful to feel the warmth, love, and sense of belonging in his home country, as well as reconnect with old family and friends. The festival experience served as a catalyst for his increased motivation in pursuing his craft.

If given the opportunity to reconnect with his younger self, he believes that prayer, hard work, patience, networking, and discipline are the key aspects to recreating success. These factors have played a major role in ensuring his own success, and he advises others to adopt them. He believes that every journey comes with rewards, and for him, the ability to heal people's souls through music is one of those rewards. Music has the power to reach the innermost parts of our hearts, minds, and spirits in a way that not many things can. Having the privilege and ability to touch people in such a powerful and rare manner is very rewarding for him. Additionally, being a public figure and an inspiration to young people is another rewarding aspect of his journey. His story encourages others to always pursue their dreams, no matter the obstacles they may face.

Nhlonipho, like many other artists, recognizes the power of social media in reaching out to his audience and expanding his reach. By utilizing social media platforms, such as Instagram, Twitter, and Facebook, Nhlonipho is able to connect with people from various parts of Africa, including Tanzania, Kenya, and many more.

Having a strong presence on social media is crucial for artists in today's world. It allows them to showcase their work, build a fan base, and connect with their audience on a more personal level. Through social media, Nhlonipho can share his music, updates on his career, and engage with his fans through comments, direct messages, and live streams.

Social media also provides opportunities for Nhlonipho to promote his music and upcoming projects. He can share snippets of his songs, music videos, and behind-the-scenes footage to generate excitement and anticipation among his followers. By using hashtags, tagging relevant accounts, and collaborating with influencers, Nhlonipho can increase his visibility and reach a wider audience.

In addition to promoting his music, social media can also serve as a platform for Nhlonipho to express his personality, values, and brand. He can share personal stories, inspirational quotes, and insights into his creative process. By being authentic and relatable, Nhlonipho can strengthen his connection with his fans and build a loyal following.

Overall, having a strong social media presence is crucial for any artist in today's digital age. Nhlonipho understands this and has effectively utilized social media to reach out to his audience, expand his fan base, and promote his music. By continuing to engage with his fans, share his work, and embrace the power of social media, Nhlonipho is setting himself up for a successful career.

Nhlonipho Dlamini's journey serves as an inspiration to aspiring artists, proving that with passion, resilience, and unwavering dedication, dreams can be realized. From his choral beginnings in Eswatini to his ventures in South Africa's music industry, Nhlonipho remains an artist relentless in his pursuit of excellence. As he continues to shape his musical destiny, his story serves as a testament to the transformative power of music and the indomitable spirit of those who dare to follow their dreams.
