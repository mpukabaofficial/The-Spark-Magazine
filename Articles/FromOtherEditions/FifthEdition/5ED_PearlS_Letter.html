A Letter To The You Who Deserves Better

By Pearl S.

Love, in its many forms is the most powerful thing on earth. You may think it is money but people attain all the money in the world and still make horrendous decisions because of love. We need to stop being dismissive of love, genuine and true love. At the very least, we just might need to stop pretending that we do not need it. It matters because it is in the absence of love where manipulation and deception thrive. Many have never received it from anyone, not parents or friends and therefore fall into the hands of predators. As we look to start a new chapter in our lives, we owe it to ourselves to be wiser than our former selves in life. We owe it to ourselves to not be convinced that we are meant to be in places we are not valued.

Be careful of people who desire your sole dependency on them. Be careful of those who are pleased with you in bondage and never want you to experience the liberty of a sound mind. Be careful of manipulation where your every opinion is shut down until you believe that your words are worth nothing. Be careful of those who shamelessly twist the truth before your very eyes. Be careful of those who easily convince you about what you like, who you like, who you are regardless of the insistence in your heart that, that is not true. Try not to guilted to remain in ugly things for other’s sake, a well adjusted you, will be of better help to those people. Be careful of people who laugh at your vision for a better future. If all these people are unavoidable, distance yourself and withdraw their influence from your heart.

This is to you. You deserve dignity and respect. You owe it to yourself to grow, to evolve, to understand better. When you speak, you are worth being heard, and you owe it to yourself to receive constructive correction with grace, in that way you grow. A better life is not beyond you because of where you are in life or where you come from. Other people’s limitations are not your limitations. Other people’s experience will forever be your experience if you never take a step to try things for yourself. No one owes you a better life. If you never get up and take hold of your life, you will remain a dreamer until the day you die. Fear is not your friend. A place that kills your voice, even your inner will, is not a place you should be, there are not lessons there, leave. The people you perceive to be successful and carefree are hard workers behind the scenes where your eyes have no reach. Pray and read the word of God. Spend time with the Lord, know Him in the secret place so that you recognise His presence in your daily life. I write this last so that you remember it first.

You can live a better life, get better friends and a better work environment.

~ With Love
Pearl S
