Scoops and Giggles: Zandile Mabaso's Creative Journey to Ice Cream Success

By Lusanda King

Zandile Mabaso's story is one of creativity, passion, and a love for the simple pleasures in life. Born and raised in Manzini, Zandile's journey to becoming the founder of Scoops and Giggles, a flourishing ice cream business, is a testament to the power of following one's heart and embracing the things that bring joy and fulfillment.

Growing up in the vibrant community of Manzini and attending Mjingo High School, Zandile's early years were coloured by her love for creativity, design, and her horticulturist family background. The seeds of her future enterprise were sown during her time at Limkokwing University of Creative Technology, where she graduated in 2017 with an associate degree in creative design. It was during her studies that she honed her skills and developed a keen eye for aesthetics, an attribute that would later become a defining feature of Scoops and Giggles. After completing her education, Zandile ventured into the corporate world, dedicating two years to her career before ultimately deciding to shift her focus to the business she had started during that time. This transition marked the beginning of an exciting chapter in her life, one that would see her channelling her diverse interests and passions into the creation of a thriving ice cream brand.

Zandile's array of interests extends far beyond the realm of design and business. She admits to being a "sucker for interiors" and finds joy in designing spaces that exude warmth and beauty. Her love for do-it-yourself (DIY) projects has seen her infuse her personal touch into every aspect of her life, from crafting unique home d√©cor to adding a creative spin to her business endeavours. In addition to her passion for design, Zandile's love for yoga and nature is a fundamental part of her daily routine. Taking leisurely walks up the valley in her area provides her with a sense of tranquillity and helps to clear her mind, preparing her for the challenges and triumphs of entrepreneurship. Her connection to nature runs even deeper, as she is the daughter of a horticulturist and has a strong affinity for gardening. It is this connection to the earth that has played a pivotal role in the development of Scoops and Giggles.

Drawing on her roots and upbringing, Zandile reveals that her family's involvement in horticulture and farming has deeply influenced her approach to business. The presence of orchards at home meant that there was often an abundance of surplus fruit left over from their sales. Rather than allowing this bounty to go to waste, Zandile's creative spirit led her to concoct a delicious solution: homemade ice cream that incorporated the fresh, seasonal fruits grown by her family. This innovative idea became the foundation of Scoops and Giggles, a business that not only repurposes surplus fruit but also brings joy to the local community. Zandile's commitment to sustainability and her eagerness to avoid unnecessary waste have elevated her business from being solely a culinary endeavour to a force for positive change in the community.

Scoops and Smiles has been met with resounding success, and its popularity continues to grow. Zandile's dedication to using locally sourced, fresh ingredients has struck a chord with customers who appreciate the authenticity and care that goes into each scoop of her artisanal ice cream. The vibrant flavours, inspired by the changing seasons and the bountiful produce of the region, provide a sensory experience that leaves patrons craving more. Entrepreneurship is often depicted as a journey of perseverance and dedication, and for this ice cream maven, it was no exception. While her business has seen remarkable success, it wasn't without its fair share of trials and errors. As an avid reader and a collector of cookbooks, she delved into the pages of these culinary compendiums in search of inspiration and information. It was during one of these literary explorations that she stumbled upon a recipe for homemade ice cream. Upon trying it out, she found the homemade version to be exceptionally creamy and delightful, surpassing the quality of store-bought varieties by a significant margin. This revelation sparked a profound love for the art of crafting ice cream.

Encouraged by the resoundingly positive feedback from friends and family who had sampled her homemade delights, she embarked on an extensive quest to enhance her knowledge of the craft. Her pursuit led her to diligently research the intricate process of making ice cream, with a particular emphasis on maintaining its freshness without resorting to preservatives or stabilizers, to ensure a clean and pure final product. It was a quest driven by a desire to create ice cream that was not only of unparalleled quality but also exceptionally friendly to consumers. Through meticulous selection of high-quality ingredients and a steadfast commitment to maintaining the purity of her creations, she has succeeded in producing ice cream that embodies a level of excellence and cleanliness that resonates deeply with discerning consumers.

An additional driving force in her journey was her daughter's early dietary restrictions, particularly with eggs. This prompted Zandile to formulate her ice cream recipes without eggs, ensuring that her products catered to a wider range of dietary needs. This emphasis on inclusivity not only sets her ice cream apart but also reflects her earnest desire to provide a product that can be enjoyed by all. Furthermore, Zandile's commitment to quality extends to the manufacturing process, which takes place entirely within her home. This hands-on approach allows her to meticulously oversee every aspect of production, ensuring that each batch meets her exacting standards. By maintaining control over the entire manufacturing process, she upholds the integrity of her carefully crafted creations, thereby underlining the genuine care that goes into every scoop of her ice cream.

While her dedication to perfecting her craft and creating exceptional ice cream is evident, the process of registering her company was no small feat. Zandile openly admits that dealing with government offices was not a task she relished. Despite the challenges, she persevered and successfully navigated the bureaucratic process, demonstrating her unwavering determination to bring her vision to life. The name of her company, "Scoops and Giggles," holds a special place in Zandile's heart, as it was her daughter who came up with it. This delightful and whimsical name perfectly encapsulates the essence of the business, reflecting the joy and pleasure that come from savouring her delectable ice cream creations. The name not only embodies the light-hearted enjoyment that her products bring but also aligns with her mission of creating a product that fosters moments of happiness and fulfilment for all who indulge in a scooping of her delightful treats.

It's clear that Zandile's commitment and dedication are truly remarkable, juggling multiple roles within the company. While this level of involvement can undoubtedly be demanding, her unwavering determination and passion for the business are driving forces behind Scoops and Giggles' success. Her ability to wear multiple hats, from marketing director to admin, social media manager, and accountant, showcases her remarkable work ethic and resilience. Scoops and Giggles' future aspirations to bring their unique flavours to Eswatini and colour the nation with their delightful ice cream are truly inspiring. Their focus on creating flavors that resonate with the local palate underscores their commitment to the community and showcases their dedication to providing a truly authentic and tailored experience for their customers.

The recent achievement of securing a partnership with Pick n Pay Stores is undoubtedly a testament to Scoops and Giggles' perseverance. This milestone represents the culmination of their hard work and determination, and it is a clear indication of the potential for growth and expansion that lies ahead for the company. Zandile's advice to aspiring entrepreneurs is invaluable. Her emphasis on paying attention to one's thoughts and ideas, listening to intuition, and having the courage to take a leap of faith is a powerful reminder of the importance of self-belief and conviction. Furthermore, her emphasis on putting in the work, maintaining focus, consistency, and seeking guidance through prayer provides a holistic approach to entrepreneurship that is grounded in resilience, dedication, and spiritual guidance.

Scoops and Giggles' journey serves as an inspiring example for aspiring entrepreneurs, highlighting the importance of perseverance, adaptability, and a deep understanding of the customer base. Zandile's ability to harness her entrepreneurial spirit and turn her passion into a successful business is commendable, and her advice serves as a beacon of encouragement for those venturing into the world of entrepreneurship.

Zandile's belief in the sheer delight her ice cream brings is evident in her declaration that once you scoop, you'll undoubtedly return for seconds. This conviction illustrates her unwavering confidence in the quality and appeal of her products, further underscoring her dedication to providing a truly exceptional and satisfying ice cream experience for all.
