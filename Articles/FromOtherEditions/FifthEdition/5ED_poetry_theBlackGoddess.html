They call her black
But all I see is earth giving birth
To a goddess, a timeless present
Hold back your pity
For she is one so witty
Talk of a silent killer
With the vibe of a winner
So don't underestimate her beauty
She is a weapon walking
Doesn't need a gun
Just her voice which is a a silencer
So beware of her barrel
Because she never misses her shot
Wait, hold up
You haven't heard the best part yet

They call her black
I knew her as an ebony
Spoke internationally
But was silent to me
A body like a topography
I revolved within her orbits
Filled all her chambers
Coded side embroided
Dressed in metal jackets
Loaded questions
They couldn't hack it
With color to be untamed
What if beauty was her name
Haven't heard the best part yet
Yes the best lies within
Cocked back and let it spin
Her silence is only captioned by the wind

She is not just black
But a representation of facts
A vibrance of different shades of color
The rainbow got nothing on her
A kiss from the sun
Make her skin glow
Her voice makes thoughts pause
The world stops to revolve
You better hold on to the moment
No need for words
All you need are actions
Let her body do the talking
But be careful how you respond
Oh You might end up drowning
Wait......it's still not the best part!!!

Colours peak into the absence of light
Melanin is our logo, not just case at night
Held onto those moment until they have swag
In the breeze
Hollow moments, I see shells of what used to be
She call them ghost I call them memories
Hair like the seas
Eyes window to her soul
Lashes be her burglar bars
Behind those in science of stars
Her voice can call out to me from a past life
And hear it as an epiphany currently
That caused my thoughts to pause
I need her touch her again to me my brain defrost
At any cost
But she's priceless I can bill everyone else
Stung by her gun, pooling for now
Have I drowned from what I've found

Wait, hold up
You still haven't heard the best part
She is black!!
