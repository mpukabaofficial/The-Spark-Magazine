You're The Best, You're The One

Last edited: 02/02/2023

You're the one my heart has been always longing to meet.
The first day I saw you was the first time I felt love in my heart.
Heavy crush I've never felt before, my heart pounded surely for you.

I tried to ignore but your overflowing tears left me with no choice but to give you a chance.

I didn't know you and so you didn't know me, if I didn't do it then it means I'll never ever do it again,
Take me I'll take you was our promise...
That is when I was proven "nobody can definitely love" if it was a sin, shame or mistake I had to live with it for I couldn't help falling in love with you.

Giving you a chance was in every path of my mind, poor myself not knowing that you'd be the tornado that will wreck my life apart.

Wise man says " only fools rush in" now my sleeping pillow is always wet, for I hardly sleep,
My day night has turned to day light.
The best is the worst so they say.

~Sandra LePoet
